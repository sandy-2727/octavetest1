Uninstall Instructions

To uninstall the application from your Windows PC, follow these steps:

Uninstall via the Start Menu

  • Click the ‘Start’ button on your taskbar.
  • Scroll through the list or use the search bar to find the application.
  • Right-click the app and choose ‘Uninstall’.
  • Follow the on-screen prompts to complete the process.

Uninstall via the Control Panel

  • Type ‘Control Panel’ in the taskbar search and click on it from the results.
  • Go to Programs > Programs and Features > Uninstall a program.
  • Find the app in the list, right-click on it, and choose Uninstall.
  • Follow the on-screen instructions to finish the uninstallation.

Uninstall via the Settings Page

  • Click the ‘Start’ button and open ‘Settings’.
  • Navigate to Apps > Installed Apps.
  • Locate the app in the list and click on the three-dot menu (⋯).
  • Click ‘Uninstall’ and confirm if prompted to complete the process.
